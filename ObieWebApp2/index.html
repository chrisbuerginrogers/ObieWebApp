ObieWebApp 2

 · Oberlin Acoustics Workshop

Tools for analyzing the vibrational characteristics of violins

A modular browser-based toolkit. Drop in a measurement file, get an interactive analysis. Everything runs locally in your browser — no uploads, no server.

🔍 Tools

🔍
Explore

Load, compare, and interpret multiple FRF files. Overlaid plots with smoothing, normalization, band averaging, FRF interpretation, and per-dataset convolution playback.

Tool · PyScript
🔀
Convolve It

Load an FRF CSV or TRF and convolve it with any WAV file. Full complex frequency-domain convolution with min-phase or zero-phase reconstruction. Play and save the result.

Tool · PyScript
🎯
Acquire

Impact-hammer FRF acquisition with auto-accept, calibration factors, overlaid multi-position plots, template support, and session notes. Settings and templates saved to your Data Folder.

Tool · PyScript
